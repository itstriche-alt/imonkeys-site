What It Does

  • Instant repair price quotes — gives accurate starting prices for screens, batteries, back glass, and more.
  • Answers common questions — hours, location, directions, turnaround time, walk-in policy.
  • Explains what you buy/sell — accepts devices, warranty info, data transfer inclusions.
  • After-hours availability — handles questions at 2am just like 2pm; never miss a lead.
  • Filters unqualified calls — routes only calls needing a human to your team (for 24/7 setups).
  • Consistent responses — every customer gets the same accurate info, zero guessing.

What It Doesn't Do

  • Book or schedule appointments
  • Give exact quotes for buying/selling — customers are directed to come in for an in-person assessment.

* Note: Appointment booking and exact buy/sell quotes can be added in the future over time as customized upgrades.
